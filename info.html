Even when it comes fitness, there will be pros and cons in what you choose for your exercise. This section will give you an idea of what you may or may not like for your preferred workout.

Weight Training

Advantages

  • It's easier to target specific muscle groups
  • The progression is quicker compared to calisthenics
  • Quick to get into and understand

Disadvantages

  • Needs a gym membership if you don't have equipment at home
  • Accidents can happen if you're not careful

Calisthenics

Advantages

  • It's a cheaper option
  • Able to workout anywhere at anytime
  • There are low risks of injuries

Disadvantages

  • Slower progression than weight training
  • Hard to target specific muscles

Despite having their own differences, there things to keep in mind for all workouts.

  • Stay hydrated
  • Develop the habit of working out
  • Always do your warmups
  • Give yourself some time to rests
